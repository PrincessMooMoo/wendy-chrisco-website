There is the age old saying; that nothing in life is free. Well, this website is free of preservatives and free for your viewing pleasure. Please enjoy.

My artwork is many things. Original? Sometimes yes. Sometimes my art is heavily influenced by other creators. And then sometimes, it is way out there. How did this idea for a t-shirt print get so weird?

I have worked in many different mediums in the past. First, it was just pencil and paper. Then I had a traditional art phase in college with watercolors and oil paints. Now I work exclusively in vectors (also known as Illustrator). Vectors can be used in everything and can be any size. So many different possibilities.

What is my subject? Well for the longest time, I was heavily into dragons and other fantastical creatures. Still am, can't help it. Once I got into the real world though, that changed into making artwork for the customer. You want a donkey smoking a cigar? I can do that. How about just a classical shield for a school? That's fine as well. Just a nice, bold logo for a new start-up? Nothing is restricted.

So you got yourself an idea. Where do I put this idea? I know many places. T-shirts? Banners? A website? Maybe even something like lapel pins or medals? I know many different size formats. Nothing is too challenging for this smart cookie.

Please take a look at my portfolio if you want to get an idea of what I can do. Also feel free to contact me with any questions. My about page also has some content that may be to your liking.
